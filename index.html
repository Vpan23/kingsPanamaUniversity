Past Participant Discounts

Learners enrolled in at least one Kiunpa Academy program on the Kiunpa Online platform are eligible for a 30% discount on this course, regardless of completion status in their initial program. Past participant discounts are applied automatically at the time of payment. Learn more here.

Those who have earned a verified certificate for a KiunpaX course on the KiunpaX platform qualify for a 30% discount on this course using a discount code. Discounts are not available post-payment, so if you believe you qualify for a registration discount, please check your email for a code or contact us.
